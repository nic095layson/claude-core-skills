An intermittent CI test failure took a full afternoon to diagnose (2026-07-11). Root cause: a race condition in test fixture setup — multiple tests/processes writing to a shared file concurrently. Fixed by adding a lock around access to that shared file.

**Why:** Without the lock, fixture setup could interleave across tests, causing nondeterministic failures that were hard to reproduce.

**How to apply:** If similar intermittent/flaky CI failures show up later in this project, check fixture setup for shared-resource races first. Don't remove or "simplify away" locking around shared fixture files without understanding this history — it's there to prevent the exact race condition described above.
